# Design Context

## Users
Personal finance users who want to track expenses, manage budgets, and monitor their financial health. They're likely individuals who prefer a clean, distraction-free interface to manage their personal finances without complexity.

## Brand Personality
Professional & Trustworthy — Clean, efficient, and reliable. The interface should inspire confidence through clarity and precision, avoiding anything that feels casual or playful.

## Aesthetic Direction
Minimal/Clean with Dark Mode primary. The design should prioritize clarity and focus with generous white space, subtle contrast, and a refined palette. Avoid overly decorative elements or trendy gimmicks.

## Design Principles

1. **Clarity over decoration** — Every element should serve a purpose. Remove visual noise and let content breathe.

2. **Trust through precision** — Use consistent spacing, accurate typography, and intentional color choices. Small details build confidence.

3. **Efficient information hierarchy** — Present financial data in a way that's scannable and comprehensible. Users should immediately understand what they're looking at.

4. **Subtle depth** — Use shadows and elevation sparingly to create hierarchy. Dark mode should feel sophisticated, not flat or washed out.

5. **Respect user attention** — No unnecessary animations or interruptions. Interactions should be smooth but purposeful, not decorative.
